Revenge of the Flies

Posts - 12-02-2020

A SciFi short with files and chips.

A SciFi short film in which a swarm of flies goes to a programmer house in search of revenge. This short film is made for the special effects course during my M.Sc. in Cinema and media engineering.

I was involed in this project as VFX Artist and Particle animator.

Technologies involved:

  • Maya: Particles animation
  • Bender: Flies modeling and animation
  • AfterEffects: Compositing and camera matchmoving
  • DaVinci Resolve: Editing and Color Grading
